About the Shop.

"Raised in a house filled with old books, I am drawn to them:
the dust jackets that call out a historical moment,
the marbled boards, the words pressed into the page with movable type."

----- Jacob Weisberg

Since 1933, The Bookworm Bookshop has offered rare and hard-to-find titles for the discerning reader. The Bookworm offers:

  • An aimable, pleasent and courteous environment.
  • A detailed and knowledgeable aid.
  • Well lit-up chamber as to help you with the right choice to try before you buy it.
Our hours of services are: 9 am to 9 pm everyday.

Upcoming Events.

  • The Wednesday Evening Book Review meets, appropriately, on Wednesday evenings at 7 pm for coffee and a round-table discussion. Call the Bookworm for information on joining the group.
